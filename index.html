How to Play

  • 📍 You are X and the computer is O
  • 🎮 Click any empty square to place your X
  • 🎯 Get three X's in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win
  • 🤖 The computer will automatically make its move after you
  • 🔄 Click "Play Again" at any time to restart the game
